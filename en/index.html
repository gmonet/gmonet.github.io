My research

I study how water molecules organize and flow in nanochannels. At the nanometer scale (one millionth of a millimeter), water can no longer be considered a homogeneous fluid. This is called nanofluidics. Water must be studied as a molecular system in order to properly describe its properties. This approach is essential to understand and reproduce some amazing functionalities of biological systems, such as the learning capacity of neural systems.
